Who Are We?

Camp PEACH is a summer camp designed for children ages 4–7. We offer fun, engaging, and educational activities that encourage creativity, teamwork, and adventure in a safe and nurturing environment.

From arts and crafts to outdoor adventures, Camp PEACH offers a wide range of activities that help children learn, explore, and make new friends.
